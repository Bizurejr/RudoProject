About Us

The Rudo Project is a non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing and promoting the livelihood of children in Zimbabwe, Africa. We hope to provide kids and schools with food, books, transportation, school supplies, clothing, medicine etc. We want to ensure that every child has a fair opportunity for success, with your help!
